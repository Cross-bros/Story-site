General Gist of the Site


This site was made rather sporadically as a silly idea to allow me to store links to the story I am working on and on future stories potentially.

I don't intend to make some super fancy, super amazing and super well designed site, no. I wanted to make something simple. Keeping it simple allows me to edit it after likely very long breaks between any update. People forget things after all.

Maybe one day I'll have this site looking more professional. Hey, maybe I might even overhaul it completely so that multiple author friends of mine, or friends of mine doing other things like coding, engineering, carpentry, etc, can have a page on it, who knows, time will tell.

Until then, I won't say anything silly like "enjoy your stay!" or something along those times, again if anyone this isn't intended for; stumbles upon this site, it atleast will be navigable, functional, and at the very least, usuable, then that is enough for me.
